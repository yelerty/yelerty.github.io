This application ("the App") is a free, offline iOS app that provides real-time subway arrival information for the nearest station based on the user's current location.

1. Information We Collect

- Location Data: The App uses the device's location services (e.g., GPS) to determine the nearest subway station.
- No other personal information (such as name, email, or contact details) is collected.

3. Data Retention and Processing
